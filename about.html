灰塵魚 / Dust Fish

獨立接案超過十年，曾專注於出版品插畫繪製，至2021年起，將累積許久的插畫技巧帶入更廣泛的設計項目，如主視覺設計、包裝設計、禮品設計、角色IP設計。堅持用溫暖的插畫說好故事、做好設計。

With over a decade of experience as a freelance illustrator, I initially focused on creating illustrations for publications. Since 2021, I have expanded my expertise to a wider range of design projects, including key visual design, packaging design, gift design, and character IP development. I am committed to telling meaningful stories and crafting thoughtful designs through warm and heartfelt illustrations.
